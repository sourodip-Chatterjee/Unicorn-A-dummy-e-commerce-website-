Our Roots

Born from a love of the earth, woven for the modern spirit.

Fashion that breathes.

At Unicorn, we believe that magic isn't a fairy tale—it's found in the quiet moments of nature. We started with a simple idea: women's clothing should feel as free and grounding as a walk through a forest.

Every piece in our collection is crafted using ethically sourced, organic materials like breathable linen, wild cotton, and bamboo. We partner directly with artisan communities to ensure fair wages and minimal environmental impact, bringing you ethereal styles that leave a lighter footprint on our planet.

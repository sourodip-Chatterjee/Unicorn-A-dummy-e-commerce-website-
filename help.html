How can we help you? 🌱

Return & Refund Policy (Canada)

We want you to love your natural wardrobe. If a leaf doesn't quite fit your branch, we are here to help.

30-Day Happiness Guarantee

In accordance with Canadian consumer protection guidelines, you have 30 calendar days from the date of delivery to return any unworn, unwashed items with their original eco-tags attached.

Refund Process

  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method in CAD.
  • Please allow 5-7 business days for your Canadian bank or credit card provider to process the transaction.
  • Original shipping fees (if applicable) are non-refundable.

Eco-Friendly Return Shipping

To minimize our carbon footprint, we provide a pre-paid, consolidated return label via Canada Post for a flat fee of $8.00 CAD, which will be deducted from your total refund amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your dyes completely natural?
Yes! We strictly use plant-based, non-toxic dyes derived from roots, leaves, and bark. Because of this, slight color variations are normal and make each piece unique.
Do you ship outside of Canada?
Currently, we are focusing on serving our local Canadian community to keep our shipping emissions as low as possible. We plan to expand to the US next year!
How do I apply a discount code?
You can apply your promo code (like NATURE20) directly in your Basket before proceeding to the secure checkout page.
